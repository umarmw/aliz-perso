Our Coverage Options

Auto Liability

Mandatory coverage for damages caused to third parties during vehicle circulation, compliant with Italian regulations.

Theft & Fire

Protection against theft, attempted theft, and fire damage to your vehicle, with compensation at market value.

Comprehensive (Kasko)

Complete coverage for damage to your vehicle, regardless of liability, including collisions and rollovers.

Roadside Assistance

Roadside rescue service, replacement vehicle, and other forms of assistance in case of breakdown or accident.

Legal Protection

Legal support for disputes related to road circulation, with coverage of legal expenses.

Driver Injury
